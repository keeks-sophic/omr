MODULE 00: STARTUP & IDENTITY
- presence.hello: one-shot on connect (best-effort)
- presence.heartbeat: periodic low-rate (best-effort)
- state.snapshot: included later via Store (see Module 02)

MODULE 01: MESSAGING & CONTRACTS
- cmd_ack: per inbound durable message (durable suggested)
- state.snapshot: periodic (durable suggested)
- state.event: on change (durable suggested)
- task.event: discrete lifecycle (durable suggested)
- route.progress: periodic/threshold-based (durable suggested)
- telemetry.motion/pose: high-rate droppable
- telemetry.battery/health: low-rate durable
- telemetry.radar/qr: event-driven droppable
- log.event: durable suggested
- traffic.schedule: inbound droppable latest-wins (not a publish)

MODULE 02: STATE STORE
- state.snapshot: periodic
- state.event: on change

MODULE 03: TASK & ROUTE EXECUTION
- route.progress: periodic or threshold-based
- task.event: on completion/failure (discrete)
- state.event: on task/route state changes

MODULE 04: COMMAND HANDLING
- cmd_ack: per command (ACK/NAK) immediate
- state.event: on actuator/mode updates (discrete on change)
- log.event: on execution failure (durable suggested)

MODULE 05: TRAFFIC ADAPTER
- state.event (targetLinearVel, motionState): periodic tick (e.g., 20–50ms), droppable

MODULE 06: MOTION CONTROL
- state.event (currentLinearVel, motionState): periodic tick (same cadence), droppable
- drive setpoints: internal to hardware (no NATS)

MODULE 07: HARDWARE INTERFACE
- telemetry.radar: event-driven on obstacle
- telemetry.qr: event-driven on scan
- state.event (estop, faults, actuator feedback): event-driven on change

MODULE 08: TELEMETRY & REPORTING
- state.snapshot: periodic
- state.event: on change
- telemetry.motion/pose: high-rate droppable
- telemetry.battery/health: low-rate durable
- log.event: durable suggested
- presence.hello: one-shot on connect
- presence.heartbeat: periodic low-rate

RECOMMENDED NUMERIC RATES
- presence.hello: one-shot on connect
- presence.heartbeat: every 5s (0.2 Hz)
- state.snapshot: every 2s (0.5 Hz)
- state.event: on change (no fixed rate; debounce optional ~100ms)
- telemetry.motion: 20 Hz (every 50ms, droppable)
- telemetry.pose: 10–20 Hz (every 50–100ms, droppable)
- telemetry.battery: every 30s (0.033 Hz, durable)
- telemetry.health: every 10s (0.1 Hz, durable)
- telemetry.radar: event-driven on obstacle (no fixed rate)
- telemetry.qr: event-driven on scan (no fixed rate)
- cmd_ack: immediate per inbound durable message
- route.progress: 1 Hz (every 1s) or on threshold (≥1m delta or segment change)
- traffic.adapter tick: 20–50ms (20–50 Hz)
- motion.control tick: 20–50ms (20–50 Hz)
